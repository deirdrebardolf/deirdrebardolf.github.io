By: Deirdre Bardolf

Jonathan Dignam is a teacher and father from Commack who has always taken his daughter on rides to admire the elaborate displays during the holidays. Lights during winter snowfalls, inflatables at Halloween; they enjoyed the spectacles.

"While driving around with my daughter looking at Christmas lights I could not remember where all the best houses were so I thought it would be easier if I just created a map so this way I could easily find them. From then it just grew and grew. The list is created from multiple sources: the addresses are gathered from driving around as well as different Facebook pages related to Christmas lights on Long Island webpages, newspaper articles, forums," says Dignam.
